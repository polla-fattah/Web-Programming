# Final Project Demonstrations (Week 14)
## The Capstone Evaluation

---

## Welcome to the Final Demonstrations!
Over the last 13 weeks, you have transitioned from writing simple HTML to architecting decoupled, enterprise-grade REST APIs complete with automated testing and secure Linux server deployments.

Today is about showcasing that transformation. 

### The Presentation Format
* **Time Limit:** Exactly **7 Minutes** per team. (5 minutes presentation + 2 minutes Q&A).
* **The Pitch:** Briefly explain the *business problem* your software solves.
* **The Execution:** Don't just show the frontend. We are evaluating *backend engineering*. You must demonstrate your backend functionality live!

---

## The Technical Rubric
Your final grade is heavily weighted toward the structural integrity of your backend architecture. 

During your demonstration, you are expected to prove the following:

* **1. Route & Controller Architecture:** Prove your application correctly separates Business Logic (Controllers) from the Presentation layer (JSON Resources).
* **2. Database Integrity:** Open your database client and prove your Eloquent Relationships (1:N or M:N) successfully persisted the data accurately without corruption.
* **3. Application Security:** Prove your application intercepts bad data dynamically (Form Requests) or blocks unauthorized actions (Policies) successfully.
* **4. Stateless API Execution:** Open **Bruno** or **Postman**, attach a Bearer Token, and successfully execute a `POST`, `PUT`, or `DELETE` request live.

---

## The "Enterprise" Rubric (Bonus Points)
To truly secure the highest marks, you must prove you adopted the enterprise methodologies taught in the final weeks of the curriculum:

* **Automated Testing:** Open your terminal and run `php artisan test`. Proving you have passing Feature Tests mathematically guarantees your code is reliable.
* **Live Deployment:** Does your incredibly built application exist on the global internet, or is it still trapped on `localhost`? Show us your application successfully communicating via a live Public IP/Domain Name that you deployed yourself!
